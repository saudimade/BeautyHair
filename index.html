Our Mission

To make hair transplantation more accessible, efficient, and effective. By leveraging advanced AI algorithms, we automate graft estimation offering instant and accurate insights that save time and improve outcomes.

Why Choose Beauty Hair?

  • Advanced AI Technology: Our app uses state-of-the-art AI models to analyze scalp images, assess hair density, and estimate the number of grafts needed with unparalleled accuracy.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed for both patients and doctors, our intuitive platform ensures seamless navigation and easy access to essential features.
  • Scalable and Accessible: Whether you're a clinic handling numerous cases or a patient in a remote location, our cloud-based solution ensures you receive top-notch assessments anytime, anywhere.

Benefits for Medical Professionals

  • Efficiency and Precision: Streamline your consultation process with AI-driven reports that provide detailed analysis and graft recommendations.
  • Enhanced Patient Engagement: Improve patient satisfaction and confidence with clear, data-backed insights.
  • Time-Saving Solutions: Focus on delivering exceptional care while our app handles the preliminary assessments.

Empowering Patients

  • Transparent and Informed Decisions: Understand your hair restoration needs with comprehensive reports and visualizations.
  • Convenient Remote Access: Receive expert assessments from the comfort of your home, eliminating the need for initial in-person consultations.
  • Personalized Treatment Plans: Discover tailored solutions that align with your specific hair restoration goals.

Subscription Plans for Clinics

  • Monthly Subscription: Ideal for clinics looking to try our service with a flexible commitment. Pricing is based on the number of cases processed.
  • Annual Subscription: Offers significant savings for long-term partners. Includes unlimited access to AI assessments and priority support.
  • Case-Based Pricing: Tailored for clinics with varying patient volumes. Pay according to the number of cases analyzed each month.
